Paying the gap for women in new york city

This article by Winnie Hu explains a study of women inequality at the work place.

www.openglobalrights.org

After 60 years when the federal law was mandated for equal pay for equal work, women living in new york city still earn less than men. according to the center for new york city affairs, the study shows that women in the city earn 90 cents for every dollar earn by men.
